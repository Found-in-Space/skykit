Radio Bubble

How far have Earth's earliest radio transmissions travelled into space? The sphere marks the wavefront expanding at the speed of light since Marconi-era broadcasts (~1895).

Shell facts

  • Epoch: 1895
  • Radius: 131 light-years
  • Radius: ~40.2 parsecs

Navigation

  • Drag to look around.
  • WASD / arrow keys to move.
  • Q / E to move up and down.
  • Use Fly to Sun to return inside the bubble.
