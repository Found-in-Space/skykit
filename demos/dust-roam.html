Dust Roam

Free-roam star field with volumetric interstellar dust from Rezaei Kh. et al. 2024. Stars are dimmed and reddened using a simple Milky Way extinction law (RV ≈ 3.1), and the study view renders that same extinction column as a direct AV map overlay.

  • Drag in the viewer to look around.
  • WASD/QE or arrows to fly.
