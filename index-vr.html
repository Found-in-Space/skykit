This is the narrow Phase 5B headset validation page: one observer shell, one star field, one locomotion controller, and just enough runtime plumbing to test headset motion and progressive loading.

  • Success target 1: full 4π lookaround without obvious lag, with full 6DoF head and rig motion.
  • Success target 2: simple joystick forward, backward, and sideways movement for long-distance reload testing.
  • Scope is intentionally narrow: no pointer labels, no advanced HUD, no parity chase.
  • Use a WebXR-capable browser on a headset and press Enter VR.
